
Cheesy Potato Soup Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Yield:10 servings (2-1/2 quarts)

Ingredients:

	3 Hot Italian Sausages (Roman brand is good)
    1 medium onion, chopped coarsely (1/2 inch squares)
    2-3 tablespoons butter
    6 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
    5 cups water
    2 cups milk
    1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of chicken soup, undiluted (campbells works great)
    1/2 teaspoon finely minced garlic
    1/8 teaspoon pepper
    12 ounces process cheese (Velveeta), cubed (smaller cubes is better for melting)
    Minced fresh parsley

Directions:

    1. In a Dutch oven or soup kettle, saute onion in butter.
	2. Add potatoes and water. Bring to a boil and add the sausages in small pieces or how you like it.
	3. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
	4. Stir in the milk, soup, garlic and pepper; heat through. Add cheese; stir until cheese is melted. Sprinkle with parsley.
